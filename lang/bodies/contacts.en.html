Editorial scope

JackPoker Guide is an independent informational project. We publish structured explanations about poker fundamentals, bonus literacy, session planning, and safer habits around online poker products. We do not operate a gambling service on this domain, and we do not accept stakes, manage wallets, or authenticate users on behalf of any operator.

What we can help with

You can use this site as a reading roadmap: start from the home overview, move through rules and strategy sections when you need deeper grounding, and treat bonuses as contracts that must fit your real calendar—not an imaginary grind schedule. If you spot an outdated claim—especially around eligibility, payments, or campaigns—send a concise editorial note so we can correct the page. Corrections that reduce confusion or improve responsible framing take priority.

  • Clarify terminology or sequencing in educational articles.
  • Report broken internal navigation between guide pages.
  • Flag safety-critical inaccuracies that could encourage reckless play.

What we cannot do

We cannot reset passwords, unblock accounts, adjudicate disputes, or escalate billing tickets to an operator. Those workflows exist only inside official partner applications and official support channels. We also cannot verify whether a specific promotion applies to your jurisdiction, payment method, or account history; live partner pages and official confirmations remain authoritative.

How information differs from support

Educational writing describes typical structures—betting rounds, bonus wagering mechanics, posture toward variance—that remain useful across sessions. Operator support resolves incidents tied to a unique account: verification holds, missing rake attribution, or eligibility exceptions. If your issue requires privileged account access, it belongs with official support, not with an editorial inbox.

Responsible use

Gambling should remain discretionary entertainment for adults. Define deposit and loss limits before you play, schedule breaks, and stop when frustration narrows your decisions. If gambling stops feeling voluntary, rely on professional resources and operator tools designed for cooling off—not on chasing outcomes.

Privacy and editorial correspondence

If you email suggestions, exclude passwords, payment card numbers, government identifiers, or screenshots containing them. We cannot securely authenticate you against an operator account, so treat messages as public-health-style reporting: factual, minimal, and focused on text errors—not account rescue.

We may retain correspondence only long enough to validate and fix content. Do not expect ongoing case management; persistent disputes belong with regulators or operator escalations appropriate to your jurisdiction.
