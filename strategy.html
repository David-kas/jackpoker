Bankroll management without magical thinking

Buy-in rules are heuristics, not insurance policies. Twenty-five buy-ins might survive typical low-stakes variance if you play tightly and move down, but PLO or aggressive zoom pools justify forty or more. Keep a separate life-roll so rent never sits beside felting chips. When you take a shot at the next stake, define beforehand the exact loss at which you return—write it on a sticky note beside your monitor.

Position and initiative

The button and cutoff steal blinds with wider ranges because they close action preflop and often act last postflop. Early position requires narrower opens; limping generally signals passivity unless you exploit a specific table dynamic. When you cold-call too often out of the small blind, you donate dead money—either three-bet a polarized range or fold.

Bet sizing tells a story

Uniform two-thirds pot c-bets are easy to auto-pilot but telegraph little nuance. Mix small stabs on dry boards to fold out equity shares, and size up on wet textures when denying free cards matters. Overbetting rivers polarizes you to nuts or air; use that weapon sparingly until you can articulate why each combo belongs in each bucket.

Exploits versus balance

Solver-perfect balanced play wins in theory against optimal opponents, but JackPoker micros overflow with calling-station tourists and overfolding nits. Start with population tendencies: under-bluff when they never fold top pair, over-fold to aggression when their line is always value. Document adjustments after each session so you do not confuse short-term luck with read accuracy.

Beginner mistakes that linger

Playing too many suited junk hands, calling three-bets dominated, and chasing gutshots without implied odds burn money quietly. Another silent leak is table hopping every twenty minutes; you never gather reliable notes. Pick a stake, stay put, and focus on repeatable decisions.

Tournament-specific twists

Shallow stacks elevate push-fold math; download a free Nash chart for your common payout structures. Near pay jumps, survival can trump chip EV—ICM trainers visualize why tight folds print money even when raw equity says gamble.

Database hygiene

Tracking software only helps if hand histories import cleanly. Schedule monthly exports, verify time zones match daylight-saving shifts, and purge duplicate files that inflate volume statistics. Accurate samples improve leak-finder reports; garbage data produces confident wrong conclusions.

Physical ergonomics

Chronic neck tilt from laptops costs EV through fatigue. External monitors at eye level, neutral wrists, and periodic stretching keep decision quality stable in hour four of a session. Small health edges compound across thousands of hands.

Note-taking hygiene

Short opponent tags you can scan between time banks outperform paragraph essays. Purge stale labels whenever you change stakes or notice roster turnover after a major marketing campaign brings new depositors.

Switching disciplines

Jumping from no-limit hold’em to pot-limit Omaha without a fresh bankroll plan invites ruin; nut-draw equity runs hotter and coolers arrive in clusters. If you add tournaments to a cash routine, track separate variance buckets so downswings in one format do not trick you into moving up in the other.

Study workflow

Mark three hands per session, rebuild them in equity software, and compare with training videos from coaches who specialize in your stake. Pair study with promo planning via bonus literacy and mechanical grounding in rules. Room selection context stays in the JackPoker review; device ergonomics appear in mobile notes.

Банкролл

Правила бай-инов — эвристика. PLO и зум могут требовать 40+ бай-инов. Жизненные деньги отдельно. На шоте вверх заранее лимит потерь для отката.

Позиция и инициатива

Баттон и КО открывают шире; ранняя позиция уже. Частые коллы из SB без плана сжигают деньги — 3-бет или фолд.

Сайзинг

Ровные 2/3 банка легки, но предсказуемы. Миксы малых стабов и крупных на мокрых досках требуют ясного «зачем».

Эксплойт и баланс

Против слабого поля отклоняйтесь от GTO с осознанием. Фиксируйте корректировки после сессии.

Ошибки новичков

Мусор одномастный, коллы доминируемых 3-бетов, гатшот без имплая, вечный хоп столов без заметок.

Турниры

Малый стек — пуш/фолд; у баббла ICM меняет пассы с эквити.

База раздач

Импорты без мусора, часовой пояс, без дубликатов — иначе отчёты по ликам врут.

Эргономика

Монитор на уровне глаз и перерывы сохраняют качество решений к четвёртому часу.

Работа с заметками

Короткие теги по оппонентам («оверколл тонкий», «стилит 3-бет») лучше романов в три абзаца: их реально прочитать между таймбанками. Раз в месяц чистите устаревшие метки после смены лимита или пула.

Переход между дисциплинами

Прыжок из NLHE в PLO требует нового банкролла и новых диапазонов — не переносите автоматически привычки сайзинга. Если добавляете турниры к кэшу, ведите отдельный учёт дисперсии.

Учёба

Три раздачи в день в тренажёр, видео по вашему лимиту. Промо — бонусы, механика — правила, рум — обзор, телефон — мобайл.
